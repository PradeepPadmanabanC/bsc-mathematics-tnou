# BMSSE-11C — Trigonometry

**Course Type:** Core  
**Course Mode:** Theory  

## Syllabus Topics

### Block 1 — Powers and Expansions
- [ ] Expansions of powers of sin θ, cos θ
- [ ] Expansions of cos nθ, sin nθ, cos mθ sin nθ

### Block 2 — Series Expansions
- [ ] Expansions of sin nθ, cos nθ, tan nθ
- [ ] Expansions of tan(θ1 + θ2 + … + θn)
- [ ] Expansions of sin x, cos x, tan x in terms of x
- [ ] Sum of roots of trigonometric equations
- [ ] Formation of equation with trigonometric roots

### Block 3 — Hyperbolic Functions
- [ ] Hyperbolic functions
- [ ] Relation between circular and hyperbolic functions
- [ ] Formulas in hyperbolic functions
- [ ] Inverse hyperbolic functions

### Block 4 — Logarithmic Functions
- [ ] Inverse function of exponential functions
- [ ] Values of Log(u + iv)
- [ ] Complex index

### Block 5 — Trigonometric Series
- [ ] Sums of trigonometrical series
- [ ] Applications of binomial, exponential, logarithmic and Gregory's series
- [ ] Difference method
