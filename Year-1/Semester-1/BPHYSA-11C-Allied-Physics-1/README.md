# BPHYSA-11C — Allied Physics-1

**Course Type:** Core  
**Course Mode:** Theory  

## Syllabus Topics

### Block 1 — Waves and Oscillations
- [ ] Simple harmonic motion
- [ ] Composition of two SHM at right angles (ratio 1:1)
- [ ] Lissajou's figures — uses
- [ ] Laws of transverse vibrations of strings
- [ ] Melde's string — transverse and longitudinal modes
- [ ] Sonometer — determination of a.c frequency
- [ ] Ultrasonics — production, application and uses
- [ ] Reverberation — factors for good acoustics

### Block 2 — Properties of Matter
- [ ] Elasticity: Elastic constants
- [ ] Bending of beam — Young's modulus by non-uniform bending
- [ ] Energy stored in a stretched wire
- [ ] Torsion in a wire — rigidity modulus by torsional pendulum
- [ ] Viscosity: Coefficient of viscosity — Poiseuille's formula
- [ ] Comparison of viscosities — burette method
- [ ] Stoke's law — terminal velocity
- [ ] Surface tension: Molecular theory
- [ ] Excess of pressure inside a drop and bubble
- [ ] Jaeger's method

### Block 3 — Thermal Physics
- [ ] Joule-Kelvin effect
- [ ] Joule-Thomson porous plug experiment
- [ ] Liquefaction of gases — Linde's process
- [ ] Helium I and II — adiabatic demagnetization
- [ ] Thermodynamic equilibrium — laws of thermodynamics
- [ ] Entropy — change of entropy in reversible and irreversible processes

### Block 4 — Electricity and Magnetism
- [ ] Capacitor — energy of a charged capacitor
- [ ] Loss of energy due to sharing of charges
- [ ] Magnetic field — Biot Savart's Law
- [ ] Field along the axis of a coil carrying current
- [ ] AC current: peak, average and RMS values
- [ ] Power factor and current values in an AC circuit
- [ ] Circuit control and protective devices

### Block 5 — Geometrical Optics
- [ ] Refraction — Refractive index by microscopy
- [ ] Refraction at grazing incidence and emergence in prisms
- [ ] Dispersion — combination of prisms
- [ ] Direct vision prism — constant deviation prism
- [ ] Defects of images — coma — distortion
- [ ] Lens: spherical and chromatic aberration
